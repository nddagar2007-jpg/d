Clarks Group Legacy

Founded in 1947, the Clarks Group of Hotels has become synonymous with hospitality excellence. With over 145+ properties across India and beyond, our rich legacy is built on trust, innovation, and creating unforgettable experiences. From managing heritage properties to modern luxury resorts, we have continually set benchmarks in the hospitality industry.
